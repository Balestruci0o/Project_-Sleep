Solutions

5 Ways to Sleep Better

Simple, science-backed strategies that actually work. Start with one and build from there!

01

Create a Consistent Schedule

Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day â€“ yes, even on weekends! Your body loves routine, and a consistent schedule helps regulate your internal clock.

02

Screen-Free Hour Before Bed

Put away phones, tablets, and laptops at least 60 minutes before sleep. Use this time for reading, stretching, or journaling instead.

03

Optimize Your Sleep Space

Make your room dark, cool (around 65-68Â°F), and quiet. Consider blackout curtains, a fan for white noise, or an eye mask.

04

Exercise Earlier in the Day

Regular physical activity improves sleep quality, but do it at least 3-4 hours before bedtime. Evening exercise can be too stimulating.

05

Limit Caffeine After 3 PM

Caffeine has a half-life of 5-6 hours. That afternoon coffee or energy drink could still be affecting you at midnight!
